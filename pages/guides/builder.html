Bugle Call Scheduler

This tool is designed to help you set up bugle calls to play through your PA system at any time of day. Once a bugle call is scheduled, it will automatically play at the chosen time daily. All you need to do is keep this website open and connected to your PA system for it to function.

How to Use

  • Audio Dropdown: Select a bugle call or music track to play daily.
  • Time Picker: Select a time (HH:MM) to schedule the bugle call or music track to play daily through your PA system.
  • Add: Click after choosing a time to schedule the call or music to play every day at that time.
  • Start Music: Click to begin background music. The music will automatically fade down 10 seconds before a bugle call and fade back up after.
  • Stop Music: Clickto immediately stop the background music at any time.

The scheduled calls will continue to loop daily until you stop them, ensuring your bugle calls are broadcast automatically at the times you need.
