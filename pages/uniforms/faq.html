The uniform regulation does a great job of explaining how to correctly wear the uniform, some members have been confused about how to measure the 1" for Cadet officer grade on the ABUs.

Question: Where does officer grade insignia go on the Airman Battle Uniform and the Corporate Field Uniform?

CAPR 39-1 (16 April 2025) states, "5.1.1.1.1.3. Cadet Officer Grade Insignia. Cadet Officers center regular size, full color insignia light silver on dark blue embroidered cloth sewn on the collar one inch from the front of the collar and centered. Cadet Colonels and Cadet Captains may wear miniature, embroidered insignia. All insignia will have 1/8 inch of blue showing at the widest and tallest point of insignia."

Figure A5-6 appears to show the 1" measurement from the edge of the collar to the edge of the blue cloth, which is 1/8" from the insignia.

However, the measurement is 1" from the front of the collar to the insignia itself, or 7/8" from the front of the collar to the blue cloth and figure A5-2 does so correctly.
